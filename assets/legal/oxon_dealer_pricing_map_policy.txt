DEALER PRICING & BRAND PROTECTION POLICY – OXON

Purpose:
To protect brand value, ensure fair competition, and prevent price undercutting.

Minimum Advertised Price (MAP):
Dealers shall not advertise OXON products below the Company’s approved
Minimum Advertised Price (MAP) unless authorized in writing.

Dealer Obligations:
• Maintain brand presentation standards
• Avoid misleading discounts or fake offers
• Sell genuine OXON products only

Online Selling:
Dealers may sell online only with written approval from VEEPEE IMPEX.
Unauthorized listings may result in dealership termination.

Price Undercutting:
Repeated price violations may lead to:
• Suspension of dealer pricing
• Order restriction
• Termination of dealership

Brand Usage:
OXON logos, product images, and marketing materials must not be altered.

Enforcement:
The Company reserves the right to monitor marketplaces and digital platforms
to enforce pricing and branding compliance.

Governing Law:
Jurisdiction – Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India.
