COD & DELIVERY POLICY – OXON

Cash on Delivery (COD):
COD is available only for eligible locations and products.
The Company reserves the right to disable COD for high-risk orders.

Customer Responsibilities:
• Ensure availability at delivery address
• Keep exact payment ready
• Verify product before accepting delivery

Failed Delivery:
If customer refuses delivery or is unavailable:
• Re-attempt may be scheduled, or
• Order may be cancelled and future COD access restricted.

Fraud Prevention:
The Company may:
• Verify orders via phone confirmation
• Restrict COD for repeated cancellations
• Cancel suspicious orders without liability

Delivery Timeline:
Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to logistics,
weather conditions, or operational factors.

Damage During Delivery:
Customers must report damage within 48 hours of delivery.
